About Us

Roshan Agarwala (founder) lives at the foothills of the Himalayas in a small town named Siliguri in West Bengal. He has grown up around tea gardens and has intrinsic knowledge and love for tea. Upon travelling, both domestically and internationally, for different purposes, he found that people only know about the 'Tapri wali chai'. If there is anything very adventurous, they will try green tea. This gave birth to Tastea.

Immediately the 'banya (businessman)' in him took control. We started travelling and learning more about growing tea leaves and bringing them to your cup.

We present to you- TASTEA, a brand by Life of Essence. We source our products from the tea gardens of Darjeeling, Kurseong and Assam. All our products are hand-plucked with no added preservatives and are 100% natural. We claim to offer innumerable health benefits, diabetes control, cardiovascular health, digestion, anxiety and much more.

We aim to improve your beverage experience and offer multiple options when choosing a hot or cold brewed drink. No two cups of tea are the same, but we promise an extraordinary experience in both cups. We connect the lives of tea lovers and help them achieve the maximum possible pleasure out of every cup of tea.

  • Suffering from Diabetes? We have a solution.
  • Suffering from alcohol addiction? We have a solution.
  • Have cardiac disorders? We have a solution.
  • Have trouble sleeping or have anxiety issues? We have a solution.
With over 40 products to choose from, we provide innumerable health benefits. We make one of the best gifts to impress your corporate friends or relatives. We bring you the most authentic tea, from the valleys and tops of the Himalayas, from the queen of hills, Darjeeling, at its best.
