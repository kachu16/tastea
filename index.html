Why Tastea?

Love from Darjeeling

Our tea leaves are hand-plucked only from the gardens in the hills of Darjeeling which provides the most ideal environment for tea cultivation and hence, the unique quality and aroma. Quality is the best business plan, as they say!

Health Benefits

By addressing issues ranging from stress, anxiety and sleeplessness to headache, asthma and digestion, our very thoughtfully created unique blends provide multiple health benefits, while keeping intact its flavour and taste.

Handpicked and Natural

The first step in the journey of tea from the farm to your cup is plucking and our tea leaves are carefully and meticulously hand-plucked from the gardens. This ensures that only the finest of leaves are picked, giving the perfect flavour which you can experience in the taste of every cup.
